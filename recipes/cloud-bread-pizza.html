Cloud Bread Pizza


Photograph taken by Mahyar Motebassem

Description

Cloud bread pizza is a low-carb, gluten-free, and protein-rich alternative to traditional pizza. It’s made by whipping egg whites with a small amount of flour and seasonings, creating a light, airy, and neutral-tasting crust.

Ingredients

  • 3 large egg whites
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional seasonings (e.g., garlic powder, Italian seasoning)
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni or other desired toppings

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form. This may take around 5-7 minutes with an electric mixer or longer with a whisk.
  3. Add salt and optional seasonings to the egg whites and gently fold until combined.
  4. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat. Drop small spoonfuls of the egg white mixture onto the prepared sheet, leaving about 1 inch (2.5 cm) of space between each.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the “cloud bread” is set and lightly golden brown.
  6. Remove the cloud bread from the oven and let it cool for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Top each cloud bread with pizza sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni (or desired toppings).
  8. Return the cloud bread pizzas to the oven and bake for an additional 5-8 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Tips

  • Use fresh egg whites for the best results. Avoid liquid egg whites from cartons, as they may not whip up properly.
  • Don’t overmix the egg whites, as this can lead to a dense crust.
  • Experiment with different seasonings and toppings to find your favorite combination.
  • Cloud bread pizza is best served fresh, but it can be stored in an airtight container for up to 24 hours. Simply reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.
