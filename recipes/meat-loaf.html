Meat Loaf


Photograph taken by Martinet Sinan

Description

This recipe is a hearty and flavorful combination of ground meats, perfect for a carnivore’s delight. With a mix of beef, pork, and veal, this meatloaf is sure to satisfy your meat cravings.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 8 oz goat cheese (or cream cheese as an alternative)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, goat cheese, eggs, salt, Italian seasoning, and garlic powder. Mix well until all ingredients are fully incorporated.
  3. Shape the mixture into a loaf shape and place it on a broiler pan rack.
  4. Bake for 50 minutes.
  5. While the meatloaf is baking, prepare the topping by mixing sugar-free ketchup (if using) with a pinch of salt and a sprinkle of Italian seasoning.
  6. After the meatloaf has baked for 50 minutes, spread the topping evenly over the top and sides of the loaf.
  7. Return the meatloaf to the oven and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  8. Let the meatloaf rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.

Tips

  • Use high-quality ground meats for the best flavor.
  • If using goat cheese, crumble it into small pieces before mixing with the other ingredients.
  • Adjust the amount of sugar-free ketchup to your taste.
  • This recipe makes 4 servings, but can be easily scaled up or down as needed.
